Hello. I'm Beth Ann—

A user experience specialist with a focus on user research. I particularly enjoy working with users to uncover and resolve pain points.

What I've Been Working On

Designed all-in-one resource hub for the Chicago Underwater Hockey Team

Through user research, we identified key information that was missing from the Chicago Underwater Hockey (UWH) website. We expanded their content and created an easy-to-follow site structure and design.



Restructured content-dense Department of Labor site

The current Department of Labor (DOL) website is overwhelming and difficult to navigate. I reworked the site structure and grouped content by categories to make it easier for users to quickly find what they need.



Created a skin care app, Clarity, to resolve consumer pain points

We designed a skin care app that makes it easy for users to find eco-friendly, clean products that are produced by ethical companies. The app satisfies a common user complaint that finding high-quality skin care products is an overwhelming experience.
